Basic Greetings in Chinese

Beginner 15 minutes

Introduction

Welcome to your first Chinese lesson! In this lesson, you'll learn the most common greetings used in Chinese-speaking regions. These phrases will help you start basic conversations and make a great first impression.

Key Phrases

你好 (Nǐ hǎo)

Hello!

早安 (Zǎo ān)

Good morning

下午好 (Xià wǔ hǎo)

Good afternoon

晚安 (Wǎn ān)

Good evening/night

Practice

Listen and Repeat

你好 (Nǐ hǎo)
早安 (Zǎo ān)
